Employee Survey Insights for Advocate Health

Project Overview

Conducted comprehensive analysis of employee survey data for Advocate Health to identify key drivers of employee satisfaction, engagement, and retention. The project involved data collection, statistical analysis, and visualization to provide actionable insights for HR strategy and organizational improvements.

Objectives

  • Analyze employee survey responses to identify satisfaction trends
  • Identify key drivers of employee engagement and retention
  • Discover departmental and demographic variations in satisfaction
  • Provide actionable recommendations for HR and management
  • Create interactive dashboards for stakeholder communication
  • Support data-driven decision making in workforce management

Methodology

I implemented a comprehensive survey analysis framework using statistical methods and data visualization:

Key Achievement: Identified critical engagement drivers and provided actionable insights that led to targeted HR initiatives and improved employee satisfaction scores.

Analysis Approach:

  • Data Collection: Aggregated survey responses from multiple employee cohorts
  • Data Cleaning: Standardized responses and handled missing values
  • Exploratory Analysis: Analyzed overall satisfaction and engagement metrics
  • Segmentation: Analyzed patterns by department, role, tenure, and demographics
  • Statistical Analysis: Correlation analysis, trend identification, and significance testing
  • Visualization: Created interactive dashboards and reports for stakeholders

Technologies & Tools

Python Data Analysis Pandas NumPy Matplotlib Tableau Statistical Analysis

Key Findings

  • Overall Satisfaction: Quantified employee satisfaction across dimensions
  • Engagement Drivers: Identified top factors influencing engagement
  • Departmental Insights: Analyzed satisfaction variations by department
  • Demographic Analysis: Examined patterns across tenure, age, and role
  • Retention Indicators: Identified early warning signals for attrition risk
  • Growth Opportunities: Highlighted areas for organizational improvement

Key Deliverables

  • Comprehensive survey analysis report with executive summary
  • Statistical analysis of satisfaction and engagement metrics
  • Interactive Tableau dashboards for ongoing monitoring
  • Departmental and demographic segment analysis
  • Actionable recommendations for HR and management
  • Visualization of key insights and trends
  • Complete analysis code and documentation

Survey Dimensions Analyzed

  • Job Satisfaction: Role fulfillment, career development, compensation
  • Work Environment: Workplace culture, team dynamics, resources
  • Management Quality: Leadership effectiveness, feedback, support
  • Work-Life Balance: Flexibility, workload, stress levels
  • Organizational Commitment: Mission alignment, belonging, pride
  • Retention Intent: Likelihood to stay, future career plans

Impact & Results

  • Clear Insights into employee satisfaction drivers
  • Identified Trends in engagement across departments
  • Risk Mitigation: Early identification of retention risks
  • Data-Driven Strategy for HR initiatives
  • Improved Communication between management and employees
  • Targeted Improvements based on employee feedback

Segment Analysis

  • By Department: Identified department-specific satisfaction trends
  • By Tenure: Analyzed how tenure affects satisfaction and engagement
  • By Role Level: Compared perspectives across levels (frontline, management, leadership)
  • By Demographics: Examined variations across different employee groups
  • By Location: Analyzed geographic variations in satisfaction
